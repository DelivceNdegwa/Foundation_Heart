Education

Foundation Heart emphasizes on motivating youths to get education. The main reason is because it is through this knowledge that youths can get to earn, either through being employed or self-employment. Luckily the education system today is not rigid. Youths can get to learn any form of skills that they are interested in. Foundation Heart gives this oppurtunity to them by opening them up to new learning oppurtunities, by engaging them in conferences and events both online and physically where possible.
